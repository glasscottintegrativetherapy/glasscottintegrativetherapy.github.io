Privacy

Website privacy information

This public website provides general information about Glasscott Integrative Therapy. It is not a patient portal or a place to exchange protected health information.

Information collected by this website

This version of the website does not use a contact form, account system, advertising tracker, or analytics script. Glasscott Integrative Therapy does not receive personal information merely because you view these pages.

Phone and email links

When you select a phone or email link, your device opens its phone or email application. Ordinary email may not be secure. Please do not send diagnoses, symptoms, treatment records, insurance identifiers, or other sensitive health information by email.

Headway and other external websites

Links to Headway, Psychology Today, the 988 Lifeline, or other external websites take you to services governed by their own privacy practices and terms. Review those services’ policies before submitting information.

Clinical relationship

Viewing this website or sending an inquiry does not establish a therapist-client relationship. A clinical relationship begins only after appropriate intake, consent, and scheduling procedures are completed.

Emergency use

This website, email address, and voicemail are not monitored as emergency services. Call 911 when there is immediate danger. In the United States, call or text 988 for crisis support.

Questions

Questions about this page may be directed to erin.glasscott@glasscottintegrativetherapy.com.
