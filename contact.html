What happens next?

I typically respond to new inquiries within one business day. During a consultation, we can discuss what brings you to therapy, scheduling, insurance or private-pay questions, and whether working together feels like a good fit.

Need immediate help?

This practice does not provide emergency or crisis response through the website, email, or voicemail. Call 911 if there is immediate danger. In the United States, call or text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.

Frequently asked questions

Before you reach out

Do you offer in-person appointments?

The practice is telehealth only and serves clients who are physically located in Pennsylvania at the time of their appointment.

Do you accept insurance?

Erin works with multiple insurance plans through Headway. Use the Headway booking page to see current plan participation and confirm your benefits.

Can I schedule a consultation first?

Yes. You may call or email to request a free 15-minute consultation before scheduling a full session.

Is this website a secure client portal?

No. This is a public informational website hosted on GitHub Pages. Do not submit clinical information through the website or ordinary email.
